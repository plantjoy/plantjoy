PlantJoy User Privacy
Effective Date: [2024.11.15]

At PlantJoy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes the types of data we collect, how we use, store, and share it, as well as the choices you have regarding your information. By using PlantJoy, you agree to this policy. Please read it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

 

1.1 User Content and Activity

·       Plant Images and Metadata: We collect images and related metadata you upload to provide accurate plant identification.

·       Care Preferences: Data about your care preferences and plant types is collected to personalize care schedules.

1.2 Device and App Usage Information

·       Device Information: Information such as device model, OS version, and unique identifiers help us optimize app performance.

·       Log Data: We collect data such as IP address, timestamp, and app settings to troubleshoot and improve functionality.

·       Cookies and Similar Technologies: Cookies manage user sessions and track usage trends. You can adjust these settings in your device preferences.

1.3 Location Information

·       Approximate Location: Based on your IP, we collect non-precise location data to tailor care suggestions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to deliver and enhance PlantJoy services, including:

·       Core Services: Plant identification, care advice, health diagnostics, and plant insights.

·       Personalized Plant Care: Customize care based on your plants, location, and environmental factors.

·       Notifications and Reminders: With consent, send reminders for plant care tasks.

·       Analytics and Improvements: Analyze usage to enhance app performance and user experience.

·       Customer Support: Assist with inquiries and resolve issues.

·       Marketing and Communication: With consent, send promotional updates. Communication preferences can be managed in-app.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your information. In certain cases, we may share it as follows:

·       Service Providers: Third-party providers assist with data hosting, customer support, and analytics. They only access your data as necessary and are contractually obligated to protect it.

·       Legal Compliance and Protection: We may disclose information as required by law, such as for compliance or legal processes.

·       Business Transfers: If we undergo a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred, and we will notify you of any change.

4. Data Storage and Security

We prioritize data security by storing information securely and encrypting it to prevent unauthorized access. Data is retained only as necessary for the purposes in this policy or as legally required.

5. Your Rights and Choices

·       Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your information.

·       Data Portability: We can provide a structured, machine-readable format of your data upon request.

·       Deletion: You may request data deletion by contacting support, although certain data may be retained if legally required.

·       Marketing Preferences: Opt out of marketing communications in the app settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

PlantJoy is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If we discover such data, it will be deleted promptly.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16, and in the event that we learn that a child under the age of 16 has provided information on the Application, we will delete that information as soon as possible.
